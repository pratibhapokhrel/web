Welcome to ALVN Education
Consultancy

ALVN Education Consultancy is a leading education consultancy based in Kalanki, Kathmandu, Nepal. We are dedicated to helping Nepali students achieve their dreams of studying abroad at world-renowned universities.

With years of experience in the education industry, our team of expert counselors provides personalized guidance to each student, ensuring they find the perfect match for their academic aspirations and career goals.

We maintain strong partnerships with prestigious universities across Australia, UK, USA, Canada, Japan, South Korea, Finland, Germany, and Malta, offering our students exclusive access to diverse educational opportunities.

Our Mission
